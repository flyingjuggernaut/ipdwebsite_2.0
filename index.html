Mindfulness. Focus. Performance.

STIL improves users’ mindfulness, focus, and performance. Designed for both mild anxiety sufferers and everyday stress management, STIL is a simple solution. It unites three elements

  • Early warning system to enhance awareness
  • Proven, field-tested CBT exercises
  • Seamless, simple technology and design
How it works
Stil short circuits traditional stress-cycles to enable improved well-being in all you do.
Using cutting-edge sensing technology and AI algorithms to define your personalized “stress benchmark,” Stil gently alerts you when your stress levels begin to elevate. With simple cues, Stil guides you through effective meditative breathing techniques.

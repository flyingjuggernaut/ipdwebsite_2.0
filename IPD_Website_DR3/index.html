How it works
Stil unites mindful awareness with traditional practices and seamless, proven technology.
Stil works by monitoring your body's natural stress indicators. Stil uses a proprietary algorithms and data to define a personalized stress baseline that acts as a trigger point to bring your attention to creeping levels of stress. By bringing your attention to stress levels early on, Still short circuits stress episodes, reducing the impact to optimize your mindfulness, focus, and performance.
When stil is activated, it leads you through a series of effective breathing techniques. The method is to use gentle, calming pulses or visually-displayed pulses to focus your breathing and de-escalate your stress levels. This is based on the "box breathing technique" used proven through global use--from the Navy SEALS to meditation retreats. Now, it travels with you.
